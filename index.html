Mentorship

Equipping the Military of the Future

We endeavour to give our military members and civilians the skills and knowledge they need to fight and win on an increasingly digital warfront. Our goal is to make Fort Gordon's service members the most technologically savvy in the Department of Defense. To accomplish that, we have created a program dedicated to teaching and mentoring programmers to hone their craft into an art form.

Members gain hands-on experience working on real-world projects that help the community, put together short presentations on coding topics, and participate in puzzles, like Capture the Flags (CTFs), that challenge their skills.

Applying Our Skills

As a community of skilled programmers, we are uniquely able to supply other organizations on Fort Gordon and in the local community with software development and consultancy talent that can meet their needs when such talent is unavailable internally. We have lent our skills to developing software projects across multiple domains for such organizations as Army Community Service, the Air Force Sergeants Association, and the Society of Air Force Psychologists, to name a few.

Join Us!

Have an interest in computer science and want to become a better programmer? Are you located on or near Fort Gordon? Join Falcon Coders today! We are a network of programmers and software developers in Augusta, GA dedicated to improving ourselves and our community.
