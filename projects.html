Projects

Falcon Coders has done some great work for a number of public and private organizations! These projects cover a wide variety of platforms and technologies, showcasing the diversity of talent we have. Check out a small sampling of our work here.


Rosetta Bomb

Rosetta Bomb is a mod for the popular virtual reality game Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes (KTANE) that we are currently developing. It is designed to make learning and practicing language skills more effective while also making it more fun! KTANE is designed to be fun but making it a valuable learning tool for linguists has required some creativity in designing fruitful activities for players that work within the game. Development has been done in the C# programming language for the Unity Game Engine.

AFSA Website

As a community of skilled programmers, we are uniquely able to supply other organizations on Fort Gordon and in the local community with software development and consultancy talent that can meet their needs when such talent is unavailable internally. We have lent our skills to developing software projects across multiple domains for such organizations as Army Community Service, the Air Force Sergeants Association, and the Society of Air Force Psychologists, to name a few.
